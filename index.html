Betelgeuse

Betelgeuse is usually the tenth-brightest star in the night sky and, after Rigel, the second-brightest in the constellation of Orion.

Rigel

Rigel is a blue supergiant that is the brightest star in the constellation Orion (the Hunter).

Altair

Altair is a bright star in the northern summer sky that is one of the three stars that form an astronomical asterism called the Summer Triangle.
